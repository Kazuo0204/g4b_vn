16/October/2020

Announcement of Listing on Tokyo Stock Exchange Mothers

(Market of the high-growth and emerging stocks)

We are pleased to announce the successful listing of giftee Inc. (Head office: Shinagawa-Ku, Tokyo; Representative Director: Mutsumi Ota) on the Tokyo Stock Exchange Mothers on the 20th September 2019. On this auspicious occasion, we would like to take this opportunity to express our sincere appreciation to our service users, clients, shareholders and other stakeholders for their valuable support.

Under the mission of “connecting people & people”, “people & business”, and “people & community” by gifting, our group of companies are looking forward to expand the business of e-gift platform that provides a total end-to-end solution from e-gift issuance to distribution. Moving forward, we will continue to work on business expansion, to cultivate a new culture and bring about new values to the world through the e-gift platform. We shall strive to continuously improve our corporate value and we hope to continue to receive your unwavering support.
