Quà tặng tốt, khuyến mãi lớn

Cách tặng quà điện tử tiện lợi đảm bảo sự hài lòng của khách hàng.

01 "eGift Catalogue" for your customer's favourite

Recipient / end-user can choose a favorite E-Voucher in the Catalogue and
it brings higer customer satisfaction without inventory management.

02 "giftee Delivery Dashboard" to support the distribution

giftee will provide Delivery Dashboard to deliver E-Vouchers to consumer via SMS / email.
It's user friendly to use and no need to disclose the privacy data.

03 "giftee API" for real-time E-Voucher generation

giftee API allows corporate’s systems to generate more than 50 E-Vouchers real-time by system integration.
It's highly recommended for long-term promotion.
